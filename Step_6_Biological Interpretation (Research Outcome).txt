Biological Interpretation (Research Outcome)
1. What does this sequence likely do?
The sequence codes for the Intraflagellar Transport Protein 20 (IFT20). This protein is a critical component of the intraflagellar transport system, which acts like a "freight train" for cells. It is responsible for moving proteins from the cell body into the cilium (a hair-like structure on the cell surface used for signaling and movement). Specifically, IFT20 is known to traffic membrane proteins to the ciliary tip.
